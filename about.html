Hi, I'm Chidera Promise Ugboaja

I'm 16 years old, based in Bariga, Lagos, and I built DrainCheck NG to give my community a voice in preventing floods and other environmental dangers.

Why I Built DrainCheck NG

Three months ago, there was heavy rain in Bariga. The gutters on my aunt's street had been clogged for weeks - plastic bottles, food waste, everything just piling up. Nobody reported it. Nobody cleared it.

When the rain came, her entire shop flooded. She sells foodstuffs - rice, beans, garri, provisions. Everything got soaked. Her fridge stopped working. Goods she'd spent months saving to buy were ruined in one night.

I watched her try to salvage what she could, water up to her ankles, and I kept thinking: "We all saw those blocked gutters. We walked past them every day. Why didn't anyone clear or report it before this happened?"

That's when I realized the problem wasn't that people don't care. The problem is we don't have simple tools to turn what we see into action that prevents disaster.

DrainCheck NG is my answer to that night.

Our Mission

DrainCheck NG exists to bridge the gap between residents who see blocked drains daily and organizations equipped to clear them. We believe early reporting prevents disasters.
